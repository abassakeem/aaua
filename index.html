Introduction

Dear colleagues, (Humboldtian or non Humboldtian), I am excited to inform you that approval has been given today by the Alexander von Humboldt Foundation (AvH), Germany to hold another International Conference: AAUA Humboldt Kolleg 2023 at Adekunle Ajasin University, Akungba-Akoko, Ondo State.

Date: Mon 23rd -Thursday 26th October, 2023

Venue: Nelson Mandela hall, Along Faculty of Science building.

Convener: Prof. Dr. Timothy O. Adejumo FAvH, FISDS, FRSB

Main Theme: Envisioning the Future: Higher Education and Modern Technology in Agriculture, Science, Healthcare and Environment.

Sub-Themes:

Food Security and Application of Modern Technology in Agriculture

Current and Modern Technologies in Science and Healthcare

The role of Technologies in environmental pollution, waste management, environmental monitoring and Climate Change. Innovations and Entrepreneurship.
