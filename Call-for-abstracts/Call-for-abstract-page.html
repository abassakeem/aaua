TYPE OF CONTRIBUTION

The contribution could be a review article, original article, opinion paper or theoretical/hypothetical paper that is well-rooted and rich in all matters that are related to any of the Conference sub-themes.

ABSTRACT FORMAT

Abstract should not be more than 250 words in Times New Roman font size 12, single spaced, all in Microsoft word. The abstract should contain title of the paper, full name(s), affiliation, email and phone contacts of the author(s). The corresponding author should be identified in multi-authored submissions.

SUBMISSION OF ABSTRACTS AND DEADLINE:

The abstract should be emailed to: aauahumboldtkolleg23@gmail.com or submit via the link below: Click Here To Submit Abstract.
Strictly, the deadline for submission of the abstract is August 25,, 2023. Authors of accepted abstracts will be notified on or before August 31, 2023.

REGISTRATION

All participants whose abstracts have been accepted must register for the Conference. Participants are to log onto aauahumbldt2023.com to complete the online registration not later than August 31, 2023 and submit.

PUBLICATION OF FULL PAPERS:

The full articles, after passing the peer-review process, will be published by Cuvillier Logos Verlag Publishers, Berlin, Germany.
